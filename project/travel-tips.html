Travel Tips

Helpful information to make your trip around the Philippines safe, enjoyable, and memorable.

Transportation

Domestic flights are the fastest way to travel between islands. Ferries and buses are affordable alternatives for shorter distances.

Budgeting

Local food and public transportation are generally affordable. Plan extra funds for island hopping tours and entrance fees.

Safety

Stay informed about weather conditions, keep valuables secure, and follow local travel advisories.
