Philippine Explorer

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Philippine Explorer

The name "Philippine Explorer" was selected because it clearly communicates the purpose of the website: helping visitors discover and learn about the most beautiful and popular tourist destinations throughout the Philippines.

Optional Domain: philippineexplorer.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about popular tourist destinations in the Philippines. Visitors can explore famous islands, historical landmarks, cultural attractions, and natural wonders. The website will offer travel tips, destination highlights, and recommendations to help travelers plan memorable trips throughout the country.

Scenarios

  • Which Philippine island is best for beaches and water activities?
  • What are the top historical landmarks to visit in the Philippines?
  • What travel tips should I know before visiting the Philippines?
  • Which destinations are family-friendly and budget-friendly?
